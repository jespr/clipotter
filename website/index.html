Everything happens on your Mac.

No accounts, no cloud, no upload. Just drop a file in.

🔒

Private by default

Transcribes with Apple's on-device speech models. Your media never leaves your Mac.

⏱️

Timestamped, clickable

Every line is timestamped. Click a line — or press ↑/↓ — to jump the video to that exact moment.

🎬

Watch & read side by side

Video on the left, transcript on the right. The current line highlights as it plays.

✨

Ask it anything

Run a prompt over the transcript with Apple's on-device model or Claude — summaries, action items, cleanups.

⭐

Star & export for LLMs

Star key moments, then copy them as text or as an image of video stills — with matching “Frame N” labels.

🦦

Fast & friendly

Drag in a file or click to browse. Saved prompts, Markdown results, and automatic updates built in.

Turn talk into answers.

Pick a saved prompt or write your own, choose Apple's on-device model or Claude, and get a clean Markdown result next to the original — summaries, action items, a tidy edit, whatever you need.
